Problem Of The Day Guidelines

  • Make Problems SOLVABLE.
    (We Don't Want To Stress Anyone OUT!)
  • Don't use innapropriate language in your descriptions
  • Do not enter problems with complicated answers formats. EX. Inetgrals, Derivatives, Sigma Notation . . . etc.
  • Solutions are CASE-SENSITIVE. Make sure not to add unneccesary spaces or characters.
  • Have Fun With The Problems!

Example

Problem: 2x + 25 = 5
Description: Find x. Enter the value of x as an integer.
Solution: -10
