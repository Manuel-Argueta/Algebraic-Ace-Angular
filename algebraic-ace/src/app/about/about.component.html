Why Algebraic Ace?

I decided to create Algebraic Ace in order to spread the love that I have for mathematics in my community. I believe that all teenagers deserve to see the beautiful and fun side of mathematics; to get rid of the idea that math is just the annoying and difficult subject at school. Algebraic Ace is meant to allow kids to not only practice some basic math skills and arithmetic, find fun and valuable resources, but also to allow them to share their experiences with mathematics.
